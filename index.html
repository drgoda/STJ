About the Book

This book captures the life story and culinary journey of a person I know very well, featuring 30 recipes intertwined with personal stories and photographs. It is a love letter to the meals that bring us together and the memories that linger long after the plates are cleared.

Crafted with warmth and care, each chapter pairs a heartfelt vignette with a dish to savour — inviting you into a kitchen rich with tradition, nostalgia, and the simple joy of cooking for the ones we love.

About the Author

Rooted in a lifetime of shared meals and meaningful moments, the author’s culinary voice is intimate and generous. With a knack for storytelling, they invite readers to linger — to savour — and to discover the recipes that shaped a life of taste.
